ABOUT US

GoCareer is mainly focused on helping the students with their career decisions. Even the institute managers can advertise about their institution. This website shows various fields available after school and also graduation. The marked feature about our web application is that, here career plans not only means education but also other fields like sports and arts. Firstly, we provide all the courses available to the user. On selecting a certain course, the user will be given all the institutes which provide that particular course. This gives details about the course along with the institute details such as address, course duration, fee structure, etc. And for the institute manager, he can have the list of users and he can send messages to the user which will be like free platform to advertise. This system will bring all data in one place for students and help them to decide easily in a flash.
